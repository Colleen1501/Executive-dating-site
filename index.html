The traits that create professional success are often the exact traits that complicate romantic relationships. High standards, a strong sense of self, difficulty surrendering control, packed schedules with little room for emotional availability.

The dynamic of a first meeting changes completely when both parties know that a trusted human being has thought carefully about why they might work together. The defensive posturing dissolves faster.
